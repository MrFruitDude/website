Privacy Policy

Last updated: October 31st, 2024

Introduction

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information in the HerSpace app.

Data Collection and Use

HerSpace collects data to enhance your journaling experience, including:

  • Your journal entries, reflections, and preferences.
  • Basic information, such as your mood and selected reflection topics.

Data Sharing with OpenAI

To provide personalized AI-driven insights, HerSpace shares some of your data with OpenAI, which powers the AI model that generates reflection prompts and suggestions. This data sharing includes:

  • Journal prompts, reflections, and questions directed to the AI.
  • Minimal information necessary to improve the relevance of AI responses.

We take your privacy seriously and only share the necessary data to enhance your experience with the AI features. OpenAI adheres to strict security and privacy protocols to safeguard your data.

Data Security

We implement robust security measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure. However, please be aware that no security measure is entirely foolproof.
